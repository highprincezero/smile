Capabilities

What Smile Can Do

A suite of AI-powered tools designed to help you extract insights, answer questions, and make sense of your documents.

🔎

Keyword Extraction

Automatically identify the most important keywords and phrases in any document using KeyBERT, ranked by relevance score.

💬

Intelligent Q&A

Ask questions about your documents and get precise answers powered by RoBERTa, or query global knowledge via ChatGPT.

📄

Document Summarization

Generate concise summaries of long documents using Facebook's BART model, saving you hours of reading time.

🤖

Interactive Chat

Converse naturally with the AI assistant. Simply describe what you need and Smile handles the rest.
